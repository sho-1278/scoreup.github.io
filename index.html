Tackle physics for jee

Start with class 11th portion. First start with mechanics , and do complete it , I know some students find momentum , work ,power and energy and rotational motion, tough but please complete full mechanics at one shot as it is highly scoring , don't start any new module till date Then you can jump either to heat and THERMODYNAMICS or waves and shm . It's better to start with fluid dynamics , as it's related mostly with MECHANICS only . Then go for heat and THERMODYNAMICS , since this module is highly comprehensive so , do not go deep inside theory , focus more on numerical part and see previous year questions for question analysis . At the end , start waves and SHM module and do take it parallel with optics .so , here is the beginning of 12th . After completing both module , start with ELECTRODYNAMICS full module , i.e electrostatics , current electricity , magnetism and all . At last comes the modern physics module .It tend to be easiest if you have clear crystal basics . At the end , semiconductor and COMMUNICATION systems make 2 questions in jee mains , so I will highly encourage you to do prepare them , refer NCERT for these chapters .
